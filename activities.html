Freshman Activities
Volunteer as a Tutor

Student Robot Club organize activities annually for freshman to learn some basic skills and technology of robotics. In December 2013, I volunteered as a tutor on circuit and micro-controllers for the newbies of the club.

Summer Camp for High School Students
Volunteer as a Tutor

In the summer of 2013, Students coming from nearby high schools came to USTC for a Summer Camp. We volunteered to introduce the basic skills and experiences in robotics to them, and showed them our robots. We had a great time with the young students who are interested in robots.
