About Me

A senior student of University of Science & Technology of China (USTC).
Major in Mechenical Engineering.
Expected to graduate in June 2015.


My name is WANG Dong. I was born in January 16, 1995 at Bozhou, Anhui, P.R.China.
In USTC, I am a group member of Lab of MCIP and the Lab of MBIT. Now I am working on a project developing a new method for fabricating compound micro-/nanoparticles in MBIT, supervised by Prof. Ronald Xu (OSU) and Prof. SI Ting (USTC）, and another project developing a goggle navigation system for surgery, supervised by Prof. Ronald Xu (OSU) and Prof. SHAO Pengfei (USTC).
I took part in the 12th Robogame Robot Competition in USTC when I was a freshman and our team won the first place among 14 teams. After that I joined the Student Robot Club of USTC (03/2013-present), and now I am a minister at the club and participated in the organization of the following 13th and 14th Robogame Robot Competition.
I like doing some sport in my spare time. And I love surfing the Internet for some technology news and listening to music online.
